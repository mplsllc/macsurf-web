Current release: MacSurf 2.0 — fixes the blank-screen bug on high-RAM Macs, loads cross-signed HTTPS sites like macintoshgarden.org, cuts heavy forums from minutes to seconds, and adds a type-ahead address bar with real History and Bookmark managers.
